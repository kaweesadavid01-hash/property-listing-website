Our mandatory UGX 75,000 inspection fee covers long-term rental viewings and activates a 12 working-day search window. For Short Stay bookings, 100% of the stay amount is payable upfront — no reservation without full payment.

Transport Cost (Rentals): Clients are also required to cover the agent's transportation costs to and from each rental property visited. Charges are agreed upon before each visit and payable on the day.
